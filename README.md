###  Iris Species
- Dataset: [UCI Iris Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/iris)
- Goal: Explore petal and sepal measurements across species
- Highlights: Pairplots, boxplots, correlation heatmap
- Report: Attached 

###  Spotify Music Data
- Dataset: [Spotify Top Tracks](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/leonardopena/top-spotify-songs-from-20102019-by-year)
- Goal: Analyze trends in song popularity and audio features
- Highlights: Distribution plots, time trends, top artists/features

###  Adult Income Analysis
- Dataset: [UCI Adult Census Income](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wenruliu/adult-income-dataset)
- Goal: Investigate what factors influence income >50K
- Highlights: Categorical distributions, income disparity by gender, education

###  E-commerce Purchases
- Dataset: [ECommerce Purchases](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/utkarsharya/ecommerce-purchases)
- Goal: Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Highlights: Device usage, location analysis, spending habits

###  Employee Salary Analysis
- Dataset: [Employee Salary](https://www.kaggle.com/code/mohamedeldakrory8/salaries-data-analysis)
- Goal: Explore salary variation by role, education, and experience
- Highlights: GroupBy analysis, boxplots, average salary trends
